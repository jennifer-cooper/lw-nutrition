    • Nutritional status (weight, weight history, height, BMI)
    • Medical History
    • Medications
    • Blood results if appropriate
    • Symptoms
    • Diet
    • Lifestyle
    • Aims/Goals/Reason for booking

    We will then discuss the latest evidence and dietary advice and develop an individualised nutritional plan together. Your plan will be emailed to you along with relevant information sheets.
